Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

What medications does Bubolo Medical prescribe?
We prescribe a range of clinically approved medications including compounded semaglutide (GLP-1), tirzepatide (GLP-1/GIP), and other physician-recommended treatments. Your care team will recommend the right option based on your health profile and goals.
How quickly will I see results?
Most patients begin noticing changes within the first 2–4 weeks. Meaningful weight loss typically occurs within 6–8 weeks. Results vary based on your starting point, plan adherence, and the treatment approach your physician recommends.
What makes Bubolo different from other weight loss programs?
Unlike app-based programs or generic meal plans, every Bubolo patient works directly with a physician who monitors and adjusts their plan over time. We combine medication, nutrition, fitness, and mental health support into one coordinated approach, hands-on and local.
How do I get started?
Click "Start My Plan" at the top of this page. You'll complete a brief health intake, connect with a physician, and receive your personalized plan, often within the same day.
